Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 22, 2024

Introduction

Akhi AI ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Islamic knowledge assistant.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information. Akhi AI operates as a client-side application. Your questions and conversations are processed in real-time and are not stored on our servers.

How Your Data Is Processed

When you ask a question, it is sent to third-party AI providers for processing. These providers may have their own privacy policies.

Cookies and Analytics

We may use basic analytics to understand how users interact with our service. This data is anonymized and does not include personally identifiable information.

Data Retention

We do not store your conversation history. Once you close the browser or refresh the page, your conversation is cleared.

Third-Party Services

Akhi AI uses the following third-party services:

  • OpenAI API – For AI processing
  • Vercel – For hosting

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

Contact Us
