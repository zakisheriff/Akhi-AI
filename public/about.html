About Al-Ilm AI

Al-Ilm AI (Arabic: العلم, meaning "The Knowledge") is an Islamic knowledge assistant powered by artificial intelligence. Our mission is to make authentic Islamic knowledge accessible to everyone, everywhere.

Our Mission

In an age of information overload, finding verified Islamic answers can be difficult. Al-Ilm AI serves as a bridge to authentic knowledge, prioritizing:

  • Authenticity – Zero tolerance for unverified information
  • Transparency – Clear references to primary sources (Quran, Hadith)
  • Inclusivity – Respectful handling of differing scholarly opinions
  • Adab (Etiquette) – User experience designed with dignity

How It Works

Al-Ilm AI uses advanced artificial intelligence tuned for Islamic sciences, providing answers rooted in:

  • The Holy Quran (with Tafsir context)
  • Hadith Collections (Al-Kutub Al-Sittah)
  • Fiqh from all four major Sunni schools

Disclaimer

Al-Ilm AI is an educational tool. While rigorous measures are taken to ensure accuracy, Islamic knowledge is vast and nuanced. For specific personal rulings (Fatwa), please consult a qualified local scholar or Mufti.
