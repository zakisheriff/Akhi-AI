About Akhi AI

Akhi AI is your Islamic AI assistant, built to provide authentic, trustworthy answers based on the Quran, Hadith, and recognized Islamic scholars.

Its purpose is to help Muslims access reliable knowledge, reduce misinformation, and guide users in practicing Islam correctly.

Akhi AI is your brother in faith and knowledge, always here to answer your Islamic questions with clarity.

Our Mission

In an age of information overload, finding verified Islamic answers can be difficult. Akhi AI (also known as Ask Akhi) serves as a bridge to authentic knowledge, prioritizing:

  • Authenticity – Zero tolerance for unverified information
  • Transparency – Clear references to primary sources (Quran, Hadith)
  • Inclusivity – Respectful handling of differing scholarly opinions
  • Adab (Etiquette) – User experience designed with dignity

How Akhi AI Works

Akhi AI uses advanced artificial intelligence tuned for Islamic sciences, providing answers rooted in:

  • The Holy Quran (with Tafsir context)
  • Hadith Collections (Al-Kutub Al-Sittah)
  • Fiqh from all four major Sunni schools

Disclaimer

Akhi AI is an educational tool. While rigorous measures are taken to ensure accuracy, Islamic knowledge is vast and nuanced. For specific personal rulings (Fatwa), please consult a qualified local scholar or Mufti.
